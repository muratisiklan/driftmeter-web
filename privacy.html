Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

DriftStats (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Data we collect

Anonymous device identifier. The app generates and stores a random device ID on your device (in secure storage). We use this only to associate your drift sessions with your device so you can see your Vault and share links. We do not use it to identify you personally.

Session data. When you record a drift session, we process and may store summary data such as maximum drift angle, entry speed, average speed, duration, and a decimated path (coordinates). Raw sensor logs are processed on device and are not stored on our servers.

Location and motion. While recording, the app uses device location (GPS) and motion sensors (gyroscope) to compute drift metrics. This data is used in real time and for session summaries; we do not continuously track your location outside of active recording.

2. How we use your data

  • To provide drift metrics (angle, speed, duration) and session history (Vault).
  • To generate shareable links for sessions you choose to upload.
  • To improve our services and fix issues (e.g. crash reports if you have opted in via your device or app store settings).

3. Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell your data. Session data linked to your anonymous ID is used only to serve your Vault and share links. We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Data retention

Session data stored on our servers is retained so you can access your Vault and share links. If you reset your anonymous ID in the app, previous sessions remain under the old ID but are no longer visible in your new Vault.

5. Security

We use industry-standard practices to protect data in transit and at rest. Your anonymous ID is stored in the device’s secure storage (e.g. Keychain / Keystore).

6. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the latest version. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at support@example.com.
